About Us

KEEPTABS is a team of dedicated developers who believe in making work more fun and efficient. We understand that tracking employee information can be a hassle, which is why we created this app to help you manage your employees with ease. Our goal is to make work a party for everyone involved!

Whether you're a small business owner or a team leader in a large corporation, KEEPTABS can help you keep track of your employees' information, such as their hours, wages, and personal details. With our app, you can focus on what really matters - creating a positive work environment and empowering your employees to reach their full potential.
